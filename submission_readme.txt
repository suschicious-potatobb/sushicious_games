Title:
Sushi Tap

Description:
Sushi Tap is a simple and addictive tapping game where you tap on falling sushi to score points. Test your reflexes and see how high you can score before a sushi drops to the bottom!

Instructions:
- Tap on the falling sushi to score points.
- Don't let any sushi reach the bottom of the screen.

Features:
- Simple one-tap gameplay.
- Endless scoring.
- Perfect for quick gaming sessions.
- Optimized for mobile portrait mode.

Recommended Browsers:
- Latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
